About Me

Hello! I’m a Computer Science and Modeling Engineer

I’m passionate about software development, algorithms, and problem-solving. With experience ranging from video game design to embedded systems, I enjoy transforming complex ideas into reliable, efficient, and creative solutions.

My journey has taken me through a variety of projects: developing an official Tetris game, creating a Minecraft port on Wii under strict constraints, and building a sports database web application during an international internship at Trinity College Dublin. Currently, I’m applying my skills at Michelin, where I work on predictive maintenance algorithms in embedded systems.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring fields like machine learning, optimization, and 3D modeling. I also cultivate personal passions in music, athletics, and geography, which keep me curious, disciplined, and creative in both work and life.
